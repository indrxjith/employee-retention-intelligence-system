# ERIS Business Insights


## Query 1: Overall Employee Attrition Rate

### Business Question
What percentage of employees have left the company?

### Result
- Total Employees: 1,470
- Employees Left: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%

### HR Insight
The company has an overall employee attrition rate of 16.12%, meaning nearly one out of every six employees has left the organization. HR should investigate the key reasons behind employee turnover and focus on improving employee retention.
